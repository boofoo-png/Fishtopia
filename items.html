Items

Main Items

Fishing Rod

Basic Fishing Rod to start off the game. This item will be Finn's first fishing rod and is used to catch basic fishes.

Other Items

Tackle and Bait

Essential Components for angling, comprosing the gear and substances used to catch fish. Tackle includes equipments like rods, reels, lines, hooks, and sinkers. Bait refers to natural(worms, shrimps) or artificial food, or smaller "baitfish" (anchovy, shiner) used to attract, lure, and catch larger fish, often targeting specific feeding habits

Good Rod

Good Rod is obtained mid game and is used to catch a variety of mid-level fish.

Shiny Rod

A secret shiny rod that can boost the chance of finding shiny fishes. This fishing rod is very hard to obtain and the only way to find it is to complete all the quests available in the game.

Water Bottle

A empty plastic water bottle that Finn sometimes catches when he is unable to catch a fish. It serves no purpose in the game.
